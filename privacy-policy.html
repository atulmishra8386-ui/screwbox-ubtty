​Privacy Policy

**1. What Info We Grab**

We might get these types of info, some personal and some not:

a) Personal Info

We collect this when you give it to us, like:

*   Your name
*   Email address
*   Where to ship stuff and billing address
*   Your phone number
*   How you pay (it's handled safely by payment services)
*   What you've ordered before and what you like
*   Anything you say when you talk to us

b) Other Info

Some info gets picked up automatically:

*   Your IP address
*   What browser you're using
*   What kind of device you have
*   Where you came from before our site
*   Which pages you looked at
*   How long you hung around

We make the site better by looking at this stuff.

**2. How We Put Your Info to Work**

We use your info to:

*   Process orders
*   Send confirmations and tracking info
*   Answer questions
*   Make the site work better and easier to use
*   Give you personal recommendations for things you might like (if we do that sort of thing)
*   Send you email updates, sales and other news (if you ask for it)
*   Stop bad guys and keep the site safe

We won't sell or trade your personal info.

**3. Cookies**

Our site uses cookies to make things smoother. Cookies are little files on your device that remember what you like, what’s in your cart, and where you go on the site.

We might use cookies like:

*   Cookies to make the cart and checkout work
*   Cookies that show us how people use the site (like Google Analytics)
*   Cookies for ads (if we use them)

You can turn off cookies in your browser, but the site might not work as well if you do.

**4. Services We Use**

We might share some info with services that help us do business, such as:

*   Payment companies (like Stripe, PayPal)
*   Shipping companies
*   Email services
*   Analytics, like Google Analytics

These services only get what they need to do their job, and they have to keep your info safe.

**5. Keeping Your Info Safe**

We try to keep your info safe from getting lost, stolen, or messed with. We do things like:

*   Use SSL to encrypt your data
*   Payment processing follows security rules
*   Limit who can get to your personal data

But no online system is perfect, so using the site means you know there's some risk.

**6. What You Can Do**

You may be able to:

*   See, change, or delete your personal info
*   Stop getting marketing emails
*   Ask for a copy of the info we have on you


**7. How Long We Keep Your Info**

We only keep your info as long as we need to for things like fulfilling orders, following the law, or dealing with problems. After that, we delete it.

**8. Kids' Privacy**

This site is not for anyone under 13. We don't knowingly get info from kids.

**9. Policy Changes**

We might change this policy every now and then. We'll post changes here with a new date. If you keep using the site, that means you're okay with the changes.
